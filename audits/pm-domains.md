## Important Distinction

The slide deck covers the eight PMBOK 7 Project Performance Domains:

1. Stakeholder
2. Team
3. Development Approach and Life Cycle
4. Planning
5. Project Work
6. Delivery
7. Measurement
8. Uncertainty

These are not the same as the three PMP Exam Content Outline domains: People, Process, and Business
Environment. Performance domains are also not process groups or sequential project phases.

## Corrections and Clarifications

- Project Work Performance Domain is not simply the Executing Process Group.
- Performance domains interact continuously rather than occurring in a fixed sequence.
- Delivery cadence and development approach are related but different concepts.
- Unclear requirements do not automatically make a fully agile approach correct; safety,
  regulation, product characteristics, governance, and organizational context also matter.
- Delivery may create value throughout a project or primarily near the end.
- Evolving requirements are not automatically scope creep when managed through the selected
  approach and governance.
- Risk is only one aspect of uncertainty.
- Metrics should enable decisions and improvement, not become targets that encourage gaming.
