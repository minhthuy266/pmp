## Corrections and Clarifications

- Adaptability does not mean accepting every requested change without evaluation or governance.
- Collaboration does not require unanimous consensus for every decision.
- Quality is broader than final inspection or simple conformance to a specification.
- Complexity is not merely project size or number of tasks.
- Risk includes opportunities as well as threats, and the strongest response is not always the
  optimal response.
- Delivering an output does not guarantee the intended outcome, benefit, or value.
