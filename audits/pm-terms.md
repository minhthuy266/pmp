## Important Correction

The previous Project Life Cycle example incorrectly used Initiating, Planning, Executing, and
Closing as project phases. These are process groups, not universal life-cycle phases. The cheat
sheet now uses a phase-based example and explains that process groups may apply within a phase.

## Source Priority

Andrew Ramdayal's slides are used as a learning and coverage source. Where course shorthand
conflicts with current PMI terminology or creates an absolute exam rule, current PMI concepts and
context-sensitive reasoning take priority.
